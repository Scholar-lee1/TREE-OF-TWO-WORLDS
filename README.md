🎮 Beneath the Surface —
Game Description Beneath the Surface is a 2D platformer inspired by Heidi Jump, where appearances can be deceiving.
The game presents the player with two apparent paths to victory: a flag and a tree. 
The flag route tests the player early with intense hurdles and precision jumps, while the tree route challenges the player later with unexpected difficulty and
extended progression. This contrast reinforces the game’s theme: what looks easier on the surface may hide deeper challenges below. At first glance, the tree
path appears simple and safe, while the flag path is filled with visible obstacles and tougher platforming challenges. However, beneath the surface, the tree 
path gradually reveals its true nature — it becomes more difficult, introducing extra hidden levels, some of which borrow challenges originally found on the 
flag route. The core experience focuses on: Player choice with meaningful consequences Gradual difficulty escalation Hidden complexity behind simple visuals 
Exploration and persistence Beneath the Surface encourages players to think beyond first impressions and proves that every path has depth — some just hide it 
better. Both the flag and the tree are valid winning options, but neither grants immediate victory. After reaching either the flag or the tree, the player must
complete two additional levels associated with their chosen path before truly winning the game.
